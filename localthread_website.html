How It Works

Simple. Fast. Hyperlocal.

Three steps from open app to garment in hand.

📍

Open the App

LocalThread detects your location instantly and shows garment shops within 50 km, sorted by distance, rating, and category. No login needed to browse.

🛍️

Browse & Discover

Explore shop profiles, product photos, prices, reviews, and opening hours. Filter by sarees, suits, kids, men's, ethnic wear — all nearby shops in one place.

🤝

Buy or Visit

Order online with UPI or Cash on Delivery, WhatsApp the shop directly, or get Google Maps directions to visit in person. You choose how to shop.
